# Leisure & Sports — Industry Knowledge

## Sector Overview
Leisure and sports covers gyms, sports facilities, activity booking platforms, leagues, and recreational experiences.

## Key Conversion Points
- **Booking / Sign-up**: Primary conversion for most leisure & sports sites
- **Membership purchase**: Recurring revenue driver
- **Free trial / taster session**: Top-of-funnel conversion
- **Lead form submission**: For B2B or franchise enquiries

## Common CRO Opportunities

### 1. Booking Flow Optimisation
- Reduce steps in the booking journey
- Show real-time availability to create urgency
- Offer guest checkout to reduce friction

### 2. Social Proof
- Display review scores and testimonials prominently
- Show "X people booked today" counters
- Highlight awards, accreditations, and partnerships

### 3. Pricing & Packages
- Anchor pricing with a recommended plan
- Show price per session / per week to reduce sticker shock
- Offer bundle discounts to increase AOV

### 4. Mobile Experience
- Leisure bookings are heavily mobile — prioritise mobile UX
- Sticky CTAs for booking on scroll
- Click-to-call for enquiries

### 5. Trust & Safety
- Highlight safety measures, instructor qualifications
- Show cancellation / refund policies clearly
- Display insurance and accreditation badges

## Seasonality
- **January**: New Year's resolutions — peak demand for gyms and fitness
- **Spring/Summer**: Outdoor sports, kids' camps, activity holidays
- **Autumn**: League sign-ups, back-to-school programmes
- **December**: Gift vouchers, corporate events
