# CRO Knowledge Base — Financial Services
**Vertical:** Financial Services — Online Mortgage Broking & Lead Generation
**Last updated:** March 2026
**Applies to:** Koalify and similar mortgage/finance lead gen businesses
## 1. Buyer Psychology
| Segment | Primary emotion | Primary anxiety |
|---|---|---|
| First home buyers | Excitement + overwhelm | Am I making the right choice? |
| Refinancers | Relief-seeking | Am I overpaying right now? |
| Investors | Confidence-seeking | Can I trust this broker? |
## 2. Purchase Behaviour Patterns
- High consideration — users research for weeks before submitting a lead
- Comparison-heavy — comparing brokers and lenders simultaneously
- Trust-gated — a single credibility concern kills the conversion
- Calculator-driven — high-intent users engage with calculators before contacting
## 3. Top CRO Principles
### Principle 1: Trust Signals Are Non-Negotiable
**Evidence: High**
In financial services, trust signals have higher conversion impact than any other vertical.
Most effective trust signals in order:
1. Regulatory credentials (credit licence number)
2. Number of lenders / loan options available
3. Free service / no fee prominently displayed
4. Named testimonials with outcome (saved $X per month)
5. Lender logo strips (CBA, ANZ, Westpac etc.)
6. Years in operation / number of customers helped
### Principle 2: Reduce Form Anxiety
**Evidence: High**
Lead gen forms in financial services have the highest abandonment rates of any form type.
Best practices:
- Label forms as No obligation or Free assessment not Apply now
- Reduce visible fields to minimum on first step
- Multi-step forms consistently outperform single long forms
- Show what happens after submission
- Never ask for income/assets on first form step
Tests that consistently win:
- Changing Apply now to Check my options — +15-25% form starts
- Adding No obligation, free service adjacent to CTA
- Multi-step form replacing single form — typically +20-40% completion
### Principle 3: Calculator Pages Are High-Intent
**Evidence: High**
Calculator pages attract the highest-intent users. Most sites treat them as informational tools not conversion opportunities.
Best practices:
- CTA adjacent to calculator results — strike when intent is highest
- Personalise CTA to calculator type
- Show a next step prompt after calculation completes
- Email results option captures leads even if user is not ready to call
### Principle 4: Split Journey By Intent
**Evidence: High**
Refinance and Purchase are fundamentally different emotional journeys. Mixing them creates confusion.
Best practices:
- Two clearly separated paths from hero
- Different copy, social proof, urgency for each path
- Test dedicated landing pages per intent vs combined page

## 5. Regulatory Constraints — Australia
- All rate claims must include comparison rate
- Must not imply guaranteed approval
- Credit licence number must be displayed
- AFCA membership details required
- Privacy policy linked on all lead gen forms
- Consent to contact must be explicit and unticked by default
