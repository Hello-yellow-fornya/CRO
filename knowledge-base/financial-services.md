# Financial Services — Industry Knowledge

## Sector Overview
Financial services includes banking, insurance, lending, investments, and fintech products. Heavily regulated with long consideration cycles.

## Key Conversion Points
- **Application start**: Credit cards, loans, accounts
- **Quote generation**: Insurance, mortgages
- **Lead form submission**: Wealth management, B2B finance
- **Account opening**: Digital banks, investment platforms

## Common CRO Opportunities

### 1. Form Optimisation
- Multi-step forms outperform single long forms
- Show progress indicators to reduce abandonment
- Use smart defaults and autofill where possible
- Save and resume functionality for complex applications

### 2. Trust & Credibility
- Display regulatory badges (FCA, PRA, etc.)
- Show security indicators throughout the funnel
- Include customer testimonials and case studies
- Transparent fee and rate disclosures

### 3. Comparison & Decision Support
- Product comparison tables with clear feature differentiation
- Calculators (loan repayment, savings, insurance quotes)
- Eligibility checkers to pre-qualify users
- Guided recommendation tools

### 4. Reducing Anxiety
- FAQ sections addressing common objections
- Live chat or callback options at key decision points
- Clear data privacy messaging
- Jargon-free language and plain English explanations

### 5. Personalisation
- Segment by customer type (new vs. existing)
- Tailor messaging by product interest
- Dynamic content based on referral source

## Compliance Considerations
- All test copy must be reviewed for regulatory compliance
- Financial promotions rules apply to variations
- Ensure fair and clear representation of rates, fees, and terms
- Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA) is a regulatory expectation
