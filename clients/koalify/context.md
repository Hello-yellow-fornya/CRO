# Koalify — Client Context

## About
Koalify is a digital platform providing streamlined business solutions. They help small and medium businesses simplify operations through their SaaS product.

## Business Model
- SaaS subscription (monthly / annual plans)
- Freemium tier with upgrade path
- Enterprise custom pricing

## Target Audiences
1. **Small business owners**: Looking for affordable, easy-to-use tools
2. **Freelancers / solopreneurs**: Need simple invoicing and project management
3. **Growing SMEs**: Scaling teams needing collaboration features
4. **Enterprise**: Larger organisations with custom requirements
