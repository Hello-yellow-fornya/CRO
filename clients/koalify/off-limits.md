# Koalify — Off-Limits Rules

## Hard off-limits

| Element | Constraint | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| AFCA membership + credit licence | Must remain accurate and visible | Regulatory |
| Rate comparison claims | Must include comparison rate | ASIC rules |
| Pre-ticked consent boxes | Not permitted | Legal |
| Lender logos | Confirm usage rights before testing placement | Partnership |

## Soft constraints

| Element | Constraint |
|---|---|
| Rate claims | Must include comparison rate per ASIC rules |
| Privacy policy link | Must remain on all lead gen forms |
| Consent to contact | Must be explicit and unticked by default |
