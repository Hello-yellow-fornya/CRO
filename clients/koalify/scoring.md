## Scoring Notes
- Use the ICE framework defined in `brain/scoring-model.md`
- Prioritise tests on the pricing page and sign-up flow
- Trial-to-paid conversion tests are high priority
- Annual vs. monthly plan nudges can drive significant LTV gains

## Client-Specific Adjustments
- **Impact weighting**: Sign-up > trial-to-paid > feature adoption
- **Ease considerations**: Front-end changes are generally easy; onboarding flow changes require product team involvement
- **Personalisation**: Segment by traffic source (organic, paid, referral) when evaluating impact
