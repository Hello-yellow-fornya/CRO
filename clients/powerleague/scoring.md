- Use the ICE framework defined in `brain/scoring-model.md`

- League sign-up tests are a secondary priority

## Client-Specific Adjustments
- **Impact weighting**: Booking completion > league sign-up > party enquiry
- **Ease considerations**: Check dev resource availability before scoring ease
- **Seasonality**: Factor in peak periods (January, summer) when scoring potential impact
