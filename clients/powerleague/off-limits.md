# Powerleague — Off-Limits & Constraints

## Do Not Change
These elements must not be modified in any test without explicit client approval:

### Brand
- Logo, logo placement, and logo sizing
- Brand colours (primary palette)
- Brand fonts and typography hierarchy
- Tone of voice guidelines

### Legal & Compliance
- Terms and conditions content or links
- Privacy policy content or links
- Cookie consent mechanism
- Payment security badges and certifications

### Functional
- Core booking engine logic and back-end flow
- Pricing and payment calculations
- Account login / authentication flow
- Third-party integrations (payment gateway, CRM)

## Sensitive Areas
These can be tested but require sign-off before launch:

- Homepage hero and primary messaging
- Pricing page layout and presentation
- Navigation structure and main menu
- Email / SMS notification content
- Any content involving children's services (parties, junior leagues)

## Approval Process
1. Flag the sensitive area in the test brief
2. Get written approval from the client contact before build
3. Share preview / staging link for client review
4. Document approval in the test brief before launch
