# Powerleague — Client Context

## About
Powerleague is the UK's leading five-a-side football provider, operating multiple venues across the country. They offer pitch bookings, leagues, tournaments, and kids' parties.

## Business Model
- Pay-per-play pitch bookings (primary revenue)
- League subscriptions (recurring revenue)
- Kids' parties and events
- Corporate bookings

## Target Audiences
1. **Casual players**: Groups of friends booking one-off games
2. **League players**: Regular teams competing in organised leagues
3. **Parents**: Booking kids' parties and junior programmes
4. **Corporate**: Team-building events and corporate leagues
